What Time Cop does

Everything your institution needs to track time.

From the gate to the admin dashboard — Time Cop handles every part of attendance automatically.

📱
Mobile clock-in
Staff clock in and out from their phones. Late arrivals are flagged automatically with timestamps and reasons recorded.
🚪
Gate mode
Gate attendants clock in colleagues. Student arrivals are confirmed and parents are notified instantly.
👨‍👩‍👧
Parent notifications
Parents receive instant alerts when their child arrives — late, on time, or absent. No more anxious phone calls.
📊
Admin dashboard
A powerful web dashboard gives administrators a full view — by day, week, or term. Generate reports in seconds.
🤖
AI insights
Time Cop analyses your attendance patterns and flags problems — chronic latecomers, absenteeism trends, and punctuality improvements.
☁️
Always online
All data is stored securely in the cloud. Nothing is lost if a phone is stolen or a computer crashes. Access from anywhere.
